Military Aircrafts

They are any fixed-wing or rotary-wing aircraft that is operated by a legal military of any type. They are divided into 2

  • Combat
  • Non combat

Combat Aircrafts

These are aircrafts designed to destroy enemy equipment or personnelusing their own aircraft ordinance.

Non-combat Aircrafts

These are aircrafts designed to carry weapons for self defense
